Quarter of a Century as an Earthling

04. September 2012

Having completed 25 years on this planet, I suddenly feel old :P and no longer a teenager (looks like it took me a while to get out of that one).

Wanted to share a couple of things I have learnt (forgive me if something doesn’t fit, I’m still learning).

Here goes, in no particular order. [Warning: Slur of clichés coming up]

  1. Love yourself
  2. Happiness is internal, and you are in full control of your own
  3. Do what you really enjoy doing
  4. Books are the best things on the planet
  5. Attitude matters a lot
  6. Never miss a good chance to shut up
  7. However solidly founded an institution might be, it generally begins to slack once its size exceeds a certain threshold
  8. Chocolates are delicious
  9. Some people are hard on the outside, but have a good heart. Others are sweet from the outside, but only because they want something. Some are just jerks. A few might be like Ferrero Rocher, though. Hold on to the gems
  10. Not everyone is meant to be a part of your life forever. Some people come as teachers, and leave once their job is done
  11. ‘Always’ and ‘never’ are two words you should always remember never to use
  12. Have fun. Enjoy today. Be happy. I know all of them are clichés but they are that way for a reason
  13. Most people do things not because they think it is the right thing to do, but because it makes them feel ‘at ease’
  14. There are only two things in life: gifts and lessons
  15. The best route to real happiness is gratefulness
  16. Always be open to possibilities
  17. Making mistakes is okay, as long as you continue to learn from them
  18. There are some things money can’t buy. Of those it can, mostly the effect doesn’t last
  19. Its amazing how much we can learn; and fascinating how much we fail to
  20. Never stop dreaming
  21. Experiences become memories and that’s all what remains in the end
  22. Destinations keep changing. It’s enjoying the journey that matters
  23. Atention to details is important ;)
  24. Sharing a very good lesson I learnt this morning
  25. Keep asking “Why?“

The excitement of learning new things is the reason I get out of bed in the morning.
