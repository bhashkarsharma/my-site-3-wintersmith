The Unnamed Poem

05. August 2009

Hi Fellas! Following is something that I came up with during the time my precious YearBook was under construction. Although I couldn’t make it very complete or appealing, yet thought it best to post it here, so it can be read by you:

This world around me,
its fun 2 b in.
As i fight 2 stay awake,
midst of my day,
rough as it is,
ain’t no piece of cake.

Define love- the toughest question of all times,
comes to haunt me. Sick as it is,
this world is love-stricken.
The existence of a new paradigm
of operability, adds a flavor
of tranquility
which is why love is referred to as
God in some places n the inexplicable
in others.
But one thing that persists
is the purity n sanctity
that true love holds.
